About Me

Objective

I enrolled in the MCAD Interactive Design and Marketing program to improve my web development skill, add value to my current position, and follow my builder's passion and heart. Post graduation, I will be seeking a new position as a Web Developer.

Background

I am a product developer driven by creativity, technology, and business strategy. My passion for products began at the start of my career in 2003 where I worked in merchandising and product management for The Walt Disney Company and Toy Quest, a Fisher-Price licensee.

Later during business school at the Carlson School of Management, I developed a specialization in digital marketing by participating in two semester-long digital / social media consulting projects for 3M and Supervalu as a Consultant with the Carlson Brand Enterprise.

Since graduating Carlson in 2010 I have worked for an audio company called Cetacea Sound where I have multiple responsibilities including sales, marketing, and web development. It is here where my passion for digital technology and developing great products has flourished.
